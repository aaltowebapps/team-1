Target device: Samsung Series 7 Slate (Windows 8)
Target Browser: IE 10 (or alternative)


An app that continuously tracks your GPS location and provides info about your trip. This is useful for people that do outdoor sports such as biking, hiking, snowboarding, skiing, etc.

Features in priority order:
1) Display current Info
   coordinates, speed, etc.

2) Plot the route on a map

3) Settings, Localization & Personalization
   metric/imperial, language, theme, etc.

4) Store the route online
   - Requires authentication (registration, login)


Criteria for the exercise:
1) HTML5 generic key APIs: Offline
2) Mobile device feature: GPS
3) Server element: online maps, storing data online
